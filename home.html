About SAST

Campus gateway to space

SAST is a campus community that gives students a practical gateway into aerospace and space technology through hands-on projects, events, and guided learning tracks.

Whether you love coding, hardware, design, or pure science, there is a place in our orbit for you to experiment, build, and ship real space-inspired work.

  • Work on rockets, satellites, simulations, and space software.
  • Collaborate with seniors, alumni, and industry mentors.
  • Get exposure to competitions, hackathons, and research.
  • Turn curiosity about the cosmos into portfolio-ready projects.
